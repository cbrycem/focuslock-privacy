Privacy Policy for FocusLock

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Overview

FocusLock is a screen time management application developed by Refinova. This privacy policy explains how FocusLock handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data We Collect

FocusLock collects the following data solely to provide its core functionality:

  • App usage statistics (which apps are used and for how long)
  • Screen time data
  • App time limits set by the user
  • Anonymous crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics

How We Use Your Data

All usage data collected by FocusLock is stored locally on your device only, used exclusively to enforce the screen time limits you set, and never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

The only exception is anonymous crash report data sent to Firebase Crashlytics, which helps us identify and fix bugs. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

FocusLock does not collect your name, email, or any personal identity information, location data, contacts or call logs, photos or media, or any data that leaves your device (except anonymous crash reports).

Data Storage

All app limits and usage data are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions We Use

  • Usage Access — to monitor which apps are being used and for how long
  • Display Over Other Apps — to show the blocking screen when a limit is reached
  • Foreground Service — to keep monitoring running reliably in the background
  • Receive Boot Completed — to automatically restart monitoring after device restart
  • Battery Optimization Exemption — to prevent Android from stopping the service

Firebase Crashlytics

FocusLock uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to help improve app stability. Crashlytics may collect device type and Android version, app version, and stack traces at the time of a crash. No personally identifiable information is included.

Children's Privacy

FocusLock is designed to be used by parents to manage screen time for children. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All data remains on the device and is controlled entirely by the device owner.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can delete all app data by uninstalling FocusLock, or clear app data through Android Settings → Apps → FocusLock → Clear Data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
